About the App

Truth or Dare is an exciting party game app that brings friends and couples together for fun and entertainment. With multiple difficulty levels and both truth and dare options, the game provides endless entertainment for all types of gatherings.

Features

  • Multiple difficulty levels (Soft, Hot, Hard, Extreme)
  • Special questions for friends and couples
  • Custom dare creation capability
  • Clean and intuitive interface
  • Available in multiple languages

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I restore my purchase?
A: Open the app and try to access any premium feature. The app will automatically restore your purchase if you've previously bought the premium version.

Q: Is my purchase linked to my Apple ID?
A: Yes, your premium purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device you sign into.
